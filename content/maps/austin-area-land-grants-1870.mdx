This map predates incorporation by nearly a century. It compiles the
original land patents — granted to settlers, veterans, and rangers —
into a single readable reference for the area that would later become
West Lake Hills, Rollingwood, and the surrounding Eanes ISD attendance
zone. The Davenport tract is shown along the bluffs above the
Colorado.
