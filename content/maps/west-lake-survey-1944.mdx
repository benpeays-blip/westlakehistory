The 1944 survey was commissioned in the immediate aftermath of the
Tom Miller Dam reconstruction, when the new shoreline of Lake Austin
made the parcel boundaries along the western shore newly important.
The original drawing is held by the Westlake Historical Society; a
high-resolution scan will replace this placeholder once the document
is digitized.

When that scan lands, the {`bounds`} block in this file's frontmatter
will let it overlay directly on the interactive map.
