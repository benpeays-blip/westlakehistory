Camp Craft Road traces a north-south line across the eastern half of
the original Davenport tract. Like Bee Cave Road, it began as a
ranch trail and was paved only in stages as residential subdivisions
opened up between the 1950s and the 1970s. The road's intersection
with Westlake Drive sits near where the original Davenport ranch
house stood.
