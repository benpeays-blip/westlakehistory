The Westlake Hills Presbyterian Church appears in the Eanes History
Center collection through biographical and church-history material
about its founding members — most prominently Gertrude (Mrs. Frank
A.) Johnson and Winnie (Mrs. E. W.) Johnson, both of whom were
honoured with Honorary Life Memberships in the Women of the Church.
