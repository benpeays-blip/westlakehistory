Westlake High School opened its doors in 1969, replacing the smaller
Eanes school that had served the area's children since the early
twentieth century. The campus has expanded several times since, and
its athletics, arts, and academic programs have shaped multiple
generations of West Lake families. A school-history collection is
planned for this archive — contributions are welcome.
