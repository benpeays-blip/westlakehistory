The Davenport Ranch occupied roughly a thousand acres of cedar-and-
limestone hill country between the Colorado River and the route now
known as Bee Cave Road. The original ranch house, photographed circa
1890, stood near what is today the intersection of Westlake Drive and
Camp Craft Road.
