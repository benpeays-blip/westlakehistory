A view of the Davenport ranch house from the south, taken by a
traveling photographer in the spring of 1890. The original print
shows the stone walls and a covered porch that wrapped two sides of
the building. The structure was lost to a fire in the 1960s; this
photograph and a handful of others from the Westlake Historical
Society photo collection are the only surviving images of it.
