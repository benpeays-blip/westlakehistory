A short news item announcing that the Travis County contractor had
completed paving of Bee Cave Road from the Austin city limits west to
the ridge above the Colorado River. The article notes new automobile
traffic on the route and the opening of "a fashionable new
roadhouse" on the ridge — almost certainly the rehabilitated Moose
Head Lodge that becomes the subject of *The Night Bee Cave Road Was
Paved*.
