The original 1854 patent for the Davenport tract, recorded in Travis
County Deed Book 12, page 45. The document describes the bounds of
the grant by reference to the Colorado River, "the Old Bee Cave
Trail," and the holdings of adjacent settlers — most of which have
since been subdivided into the modern neighborhoods of West Lake.
