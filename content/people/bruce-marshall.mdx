Bruce Marshall is identified in the Eanes History Center collection
through several photographs at the Eanes-Marshall Cemetery — leaning
on a fence in front of family tombstones, walking the cemetery with
Dorothy Depwe. Birth and death dates are not yet recorded; corrections
or additions from family members or local historians are welcome
through the contribute form.
