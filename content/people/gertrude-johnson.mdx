Gertrude (Mrs. Frank A.) Johnson appears in the Eanes History Center
collection through an interview about her decades in the Deep Eddy
and Eanes-area community, a biographical entry honouring her as a
founding member of the Westlake Hills Presbyterian Church (alongside
Winnie Johnson), and several references in family and church
material. Birth and death dates are not yet recorded.
