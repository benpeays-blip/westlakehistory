Emmett Shelton, Sr. (1905–2000) is the central voice of this archive.
A Travis County native, he practiced criminal-defense law in Austin
for decades while accumulating land along Bee Cave Road and the
western shore of the Colorado in the 1930s and 40s — the parcels that
became some of the first residential subdivisions of West Lake Hills.
His brother Polk Shelton was his frequent business partner; Cynthia
Shelton, his daughter, recorded and curated the oral histories that
became the *Our Westlake* podcast.
