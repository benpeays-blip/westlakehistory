Frank Johnson appears in the Eanes History Center collection through a
photograph of his two-storey wooden home — surrounded by trees and
bushes, with a wood-and-wire fence in the foreground — and through
references in essays about the Johnson family of West Lake Hills.
Birth and death dates are not yet recorded.
