Robert Eanes was among the early settlers of the hill country west of
Austin. His family ranched several hundred acres in what is now
central West Lake Hills, and in the 1870s donated a parcel of that
land for the construction of a one-room schoolhouse — the first
public building in the area. The Eanes name carried forward into the
modern Eanes Independent School District, which now serves the
families of the West Lake area and the surrounding hills.
