Charles Randolph Eanes appears in the Eanes History Center collection
through a single formal portrait — a studio photograph by H. R. Marks
of Austin, dated 1887. The portrait shows him in a dark suit and vest
with a black bow tie. Birth and death dates above are approximate
based on contemporary records; corrections from family members or
researchers are welcome.
