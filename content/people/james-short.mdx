James Short was a student at Eanes Elementary in the 1940s, a period
he later recounted to Dorothy Depwe in two interviews preserved in
the Eanes History Center collection — the bus rides into school, the
egg fights, the road bonfires built to stop the morning bus. Birth
and death dates are not yet recorded.
