Polk Shelton (1900–1979) appears throughout Emmett Shelton Sr.'s
recorded recollections as a partner in the rehabilitation of the old
Moose Head Lodge along Bee Cave Road and in the later land
acquisitions that opened the western shore of the Colorado.
