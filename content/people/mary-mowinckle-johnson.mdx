Mary Ellen Mowinckle (later Johnson) appears throughout the Eanes
History Center collection as both a contributor of letters and a
subject of oral-history interviews. She taught at the Eanes Rock
Schoolhouse, witnessed and recounted the daily life of its students
through the 1930s and 40s, and sustained correspondence with Dorothy
Depwe and other Westlake-area historians well into the 1970s. Birth
and death dates are not yet recorded; corrections are welcome.
