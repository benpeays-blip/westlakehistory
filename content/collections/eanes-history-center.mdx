## The official archive home

This is the **Eanes History Center collection**, assembled at the
Westbank Community Library District over many years and now hosted on
this site as its official digital home. With explicit permission from
the Westbank CLD, Westlake History maintains and presents the full
collection — 69 items spanning letters from Mary Mowinckle Johnson,
oral-history notes with the Beards and the Sheltons, photographs of
the Eanes Rock Schoolhouse from the 1930s and 40s, hand-drawn
illustrations of the 1857 Eanes-Marshall Home and the 1871 Arch
Bridge over Barton Creek, the 1957 and 1958 Travis County Rural
Directories, and the foundational books *Eanes: A History of the
School and Community* and *Eanes: Portrait of a Community*.

## Provenance

- **Originals.** Held by the Westbank Community Library District at
  the Eanes History Center.
- **Digital reproductions.** Originally produced by The Portal to
  Texas History (UNT Libraries) under their partnership with the
  Library District; mirrored on this site under explicit permission
  from Westbank CLD.
- **Contributing donors.** Acknowledged on each item where known.

## How to use this collection

Items are grouped below by kind — photographs, illustrations, oral
histories, letters, essays, and so on. Each item links to its full
record. Citations should follow the form:

> *[Item title].* Eanes History Center, Westbank Community Library
> District. Westlake History, https://westlakehistory.com/collections/eanes-history-center.

If you have material you would like to add to the collection — a
photograph, a document, a recording — use the {" "}
[contribute form](/contribute) and we will work with the Westbank
Community Library District to bring it in with proper provenance.
