This is the collection assembled at the Eanes History Center by the
Westbank Community Library District and digitized by the Portal to
Texas History at the UNT Libraries. The originals — letters from
Mary Mowinckle Johnson, oral-history notes with the Beards and the
Sheltons, school photographs, hand-drawn illustrations of the
Eanes-Marshall Home and the 1871 Arch Bridge over Barton Creek — are
held by Westbank Community Library District; the high-resolution
scans live on the Portal to Texas History.

We catalogue the items here so they appear in this archive's search
and on the connections sidebars of the people, places, and stories
they touch. **Each item links out to UNT for the full record and the
full-resolution scan** — we don't re-host the digitized images.
