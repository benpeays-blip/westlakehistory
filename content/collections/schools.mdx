A consolidated thread through the educational history of the West Lake
area — the schools, the teachers, the students, and the daily routines
captured in oral histories and class photographs. Begins with the
original 1874 Eanes School (later the 1896 Eanes Frame School), runs
through the 1928 Eanes Rock Schoolhouse that served the community
until 1969, and continues with the modern Westlake High School and
Eanes ISD campuses.

Documents in this collection draw on the Eanes History Center
photographic record (interior class photographs, lunch-on-the-rocks
snapshots, field-trip wagon shots) and on oral-history interviews
recorded by Dorothy Depwe and others in the 1970s and 80s.
