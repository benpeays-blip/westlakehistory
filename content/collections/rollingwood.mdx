Rollingwood was incorporated in the 1960s as a small residential
city east of West Lake Hills, sharing schools, fire service, and much
of its civic life with WLH. Its identity has long centred on
Rollingwood Park — donated to the city by the Western Hills Optimist
Club — and on the volunteer civic culture that built and maintains it.

This collection groups Rollingwood-specific material in the archive,
contributed in part by the City of Rollingwood and supplemented by
Eanes-area historians.
