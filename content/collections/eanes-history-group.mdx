The Eanes History Group meets quarterly to record lectures, conduct
oral-history interviews, and collect documents and photographs from
families connected to the historic Eanes school and the surrounding
West Lake community. Recordings of EHG lectures are published on the
WestBank Library's YouTube channel under the **EHG** prefix and will
appear in this archive as transcripts are produced.

This collection groups the Eanes-related materials donated to the
archive, separately from the *Our Westlake* podcast collection
maintained by the Westlake Historical Society.
