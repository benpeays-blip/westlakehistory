A thread through the religious history of the West Lake Hills,
Rollingwood, and Eanes-area community. The Eanes History Center
collection holds biographical material on founding members of the
Westlake Hills Presbyterian Church — most prominently Gertrude (Mrs.
Frank A.) Johnson and Winnie (Mrs. E. W.) Johnson — and references to
broader church-life material that will appear here as it&apos;s
catalogued.
